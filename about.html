About the studio

Independent, focused and built around play.

Angelo Games Studio is a solo indie game development studio focused on building colorful, accessible and polished game experiences.

Studio mission

Angelo Games Studio exists to create games that are easy to understand, satisfying to play and visually memorable. The studio values clear gameplay, expressive visuals, fast iteration and a player-first mindset.

Creative direction

Bold cartoon worlds, playful UI, readable action and a strong visual identity.

Production mindset

Focused scope, careful polish and a practical approach to shipping games.

Player experience

Short sessions, satisfying feedback and controls that feel natural on mobile.

Long-term growth

A foundation for future games, updates, partnerships and publishing opportunities.
